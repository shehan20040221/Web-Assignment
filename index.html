About Ceylon Crafts

Welcome to Ceylon Crafts, where creativity meets quality. We are passionate about providing crafters, artists, and DIY enthusiasts with the finest materials and tools to bring their creative visions to life.

Founded with a mission to make crafting accessible and enjoyable for everyone, we carefully curate our collection to include only the best supplies. From paper quilling materials to glass bottles for your miniature projects, we have everything you need to create something beautiful.

Our commitment to quality means that every item in our collection is tested and approved by our team of craft enthusiasts. We believe that great crafts start with great materials, and we're here to provide exactly that.

Join our community of makers and discover the joy of creating something truly special with Ceylon Crafts.

How to Order

Step 1: Browse Our Products

Visit our shop page to explore our wide range of craft supplies. Each product includes detailed descriptions and pricing information.

Step 2: Contact Us

Once you've selected your items, contact us through our contact form, WhatsApp, or phone. Provide us with your product list and delivery details.

Step 3: Confirm Your Order

We'll confirm availability, calculate total costs including delivery, and provide you with payment instructions.

Step 4: Payment & Delivery

Complete payment through bank transfer or mobile payment apps. We'll process your order and arrange delivery within 2-3 business days.
